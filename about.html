Get Started Quickly

To use this app you must allow access of you microphone to the browser. You then start to speak and if you feel vibrations it means you're suppose to speak up! This will also be indicated by a red volume bar. If the volume bar is green, then you're all set! You shouldn't feel any vibrations.

Why you should use

This app is a tool to help prepare for presentations as well as to use during presentations. What the website does is make sure that you're talking loud enough to be heard. This is important especially for people who are softspoken to make sure they are percieved as confident and to also make sure everyone will be able to hear you. This is also a a great tool to practice and get use to projecting your voice before you present. The app could also be useful to musicans. Musicans could check wether they're playing pianismo or fortismo which is just soft or loud in terms of dynamics.

Modular

All you really need is your phone! For a better experince it is suggested to use your headphones will attached microphones.
